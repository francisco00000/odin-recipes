MILK

DESCRIPTION

Milk is a very rich and nutritive meal, and this special milk have extra protein, having a great benefit for the cost.
NUTRITIONAL VALUE
  • Partially skimmed cow's milk
  • Lactase
  • Vitamin A (micrograms retinol equivalents)
  • Vitamin D
  • Milk fat: 10 G / L
  • Milk protein 54 g / L
  • Lactose 0 g / L
STEPS TO USE MILK
  1. Call a nutriologist or calculate how much extra protein do you need in a day.
  2. Drink as much milk needed in a day, drinking more than one liter per hour is not recommended.
  3. Bulk up as much as possible by training and sleeping properly.
